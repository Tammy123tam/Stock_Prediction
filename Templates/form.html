Stock Market Prediction

Welcome to our Stock Price Prediction Website! This platform utilizes a Recurrent Neural Network with LSTM to forecast US stock market prices.

Please enter the following information:

  • Stock Name: Enter the capitalized letters for the stock. For example, "AAPL" for Apple, "BTC-USD" for Bitcoin, "TSLA" for Tesla.
  • Epochs: Set the number of passes of the entire training dataset. Higher epochs can improve accuracy. A value of 50 is recommended for good performance.
  • Ahead: Specify the number of days you want to predict ahead of time. A lower value in "Ahead" generally results in higher accuracy because the model may struggle to predict further into the future.
  • Days: Choose the number of days you want to use as the prediction dataset to forecast the future.
